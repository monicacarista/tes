Glossarium
List of terms in this website
  • Avail: Availablility = Eff Hour / Total Production Hour
  • DT: Downtime
  • Eff Hour: Effective production hour or actual real production time
  • FG: Finished Good Produced
  • FTY: First Time Yield = Percentage of finish good result compared to material used in one go or FG / (FG + Scrap + Waste)
  • Hold: QC Hold, product hold by QC wait for quality decision
  • OEE: Overall Equipment Effectiveness= FTY * Avail * Perf
  • Perf: Performance = percentage of actual output per effective hour divided by standard output per hour. (Actual output per hour / Standard output per hour)
  • RJ: Reject, product that does not pass quality requirement where the material can be re-used for production.
  • RM: Raw Material Used
  • Scrap Chg: Scrap hasil - Scrap pakai
  • Scrap Produced: Scrap produced from manufacturing process = Trimming + Reject
  • Scrap Used: Scrap being used as Raw Material
  • TR: Trimming of product where the material can be re-used for production.
  • WS: Waste where material that cannot be re-used for production
  • Idle Time: Total normal work hour - Production hour - Downtime
